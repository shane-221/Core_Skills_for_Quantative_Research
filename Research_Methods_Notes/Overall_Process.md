<h2 style="text-align: center;">The Overall Research Process</h2>

<p>
  Research can be understood as a structured process that operates across several
  broad domains. Each domain is defined by the types of questions it asks, the methods
  it employs, and the nature of the knowledge it seeks to produce.
</p>

<p>
  Within this process, research is commonly grouped into four main domains:
</p>

<ol>
  <li><b>Natural Sciences</b></li>
  <li><b>Social Sciences</b></li>
  <li><b>Applied Subjects</b></li>
  <li><b>Creative Subjects</b></li>
</ol>
